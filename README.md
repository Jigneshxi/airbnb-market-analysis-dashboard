# Airbnb Market Analysis: Chicago vs. New Orleans
AlmaBetter Capstone Project - Module 4: Data Visualization (Data Analytics and Business Intelligence)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an end-to-end data analytics and visualization workflow comparing Airbnb market dynamics across two diverse US cities: **Chicago** and **New Orleans**. 

The pipeline consists of:
1. **Data Prep (Python):** Merging raw Inside Airbnb listings files, cleaning missing values, standardizing formats, and engineering custom Price and Availability categories.
2. **Dashboarding (Power BI):** Constructing an interactive multi-view dashboard analyzing listings, property room types, geographical pricing, host reviews, and availability metrics.

---

## 🎯 Dashboard Objectives
* **Comparative Scope:** Isolate market volume, pricing, and availability trends between Chicago and New Orleans.
* **Supply Profile:** Analyze percentage distribution of room types (Entire Home, Private Room, Shared Room).
* **Pricing & Area Analysis:** Locate high-revenue neighbourhoods (e.g., Near North Side in Chicago, French Quarter in NOLA) and cross-tabulate price tiers against availability.
* **Host Optimization:** Analyze customer reviews against average listing price to determine occupancy drivers.

## 📈 Key Insights Summary
* **Supply Dominance:** 'Entire home/apt' is the leading room type in both markets, indicating visitors prioritize privacy and space.
* **Pricing Hubs:** High-density tourist clusters (French Quarter in New Orleans, Near North Side in Chicago) dictate premium price rates.
* **Occupancy Drivers:** Listings priced within the budget-to-moderate range ($50 - $150/night) draw the highest frequency of user reviews, indicating optimal customer engagement.
